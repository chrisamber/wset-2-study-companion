# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-30_

Decant is built to need as little of your data as possible. In short: **it collects nothing about you, and nothing you do in the app leaves your device.**

## What Decant stores

- **Your study progress** (quiz scores, cards seen, settings) is saved in your browser's `localStorage`. It stays on your device, is never transmitted to any server, and is readable only by Decant in your own browser.
- There are **no accounts, no sign-in, and no personal information** collected.

## What Decant does *not* do

- No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
- No cookies for tracking or advertising.
- No third-party trackers, pixels, or ad networks.
- No selling or sharing of data — there is no data to sell or share.

## Clearing your data

Clearing your browser's site data for this site (or using a private/incognito window) erases all stored progress. There is no server-side copy to delete.

## Hosting

The public demo is hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/). Like any web host, Vercel's edge network processes standard request metadata (such as IP address and user-agent) to serve pages and for security and operational logging. That processing is governed by [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy). If you self-host Decant, your own host's logging applies instead.

## Children

Decant concerns alcoholic-beverage education and is intended for users of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction.

## Changes

Because Decant is open source, any change to this policy is visible in the project's git history. Material changes will update the date above.
